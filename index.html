Connecting Impactful Organizations with Highly Skilled Engineers

Despite the assent of new technologies, nonprofits are left behind and struggle to find affordable skills necessary to leverage its power. Meanwhile, coders and corporations are looking for ways to give back and use their talent for good.

By matching inspirational projects with the skills they need, OvioHub aims at empowering nonprofits with technology and help engineers give more meaning to their code. We connect nonprofits with dedicated volunteers looking to make a positive contribution, while focusing on open-source projects to maximize scalability and impact.

OvioHub only selects long-term software projects to ensure the perennity of engineers involvement.


Why Open-Source?

We believe that open-source projects have the power to sustainably strengthen the link between nonprofits and technical skills, while improving transparency and scalability of projects across the sector.

For Nonprofits

You are looking to leverage technology and software to maximize your impact? Building open-source software to scale your impact? Aeb-app, mobile-app, list tech stack. Submit your project and get access to a pool of committed volunteers.

We help you scope impactful projects and connect you to skilled individuals motivated by your cause. We select the best talents to meet your needs and ensure your projects viability.

OvioHub enables you to take more risk on technology projects by lowering developments costs and help you create sustainable code. With OvioHub, nonprofits can easily advertise projects, find committed volunteers and track contributions.

Focusing on open-source will help you build you attract talent, create sustainable tools and be more transparent. By incentivizing open-source development within the nonprofit sector, we hope to accelerate innovation share and avoid duplication of efforts for the benefits of all.
